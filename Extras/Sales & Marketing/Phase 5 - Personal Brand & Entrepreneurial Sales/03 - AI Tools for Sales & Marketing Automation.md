# AI Tools for Sales & Marketing Automation

## 1. Core Concepts

- Leverage, not replacement: Use Artificial Intelligence (AI) to draft, summarize, personalize, and analyze—humans keep judgment and quality.
- Data hygiene: Great automations require clean inputs (Contacts, fields, opt-in status).
- Guardrails: Review outputs, keep brand voice, and avoid hallucinations.

## 2. Use Cases & Examples

- Research: Summarize accounts, extract pain signals from news/jobs.
- Copy: Draft email variants and social posts; personalize first lines.
- Enablement: Summarize calls and create action items.
- Analytics: Classify replies, score leads, surface intent signals.

## 3. Actionable Techniques & Tools

- Templates and prompts: Maintain a library of prompts/snippets for emails, posts, and follow-ups.
- Workflow: Human-in-the-loop review for outbound messages and high-stakes content.
- Integrations: Zapier/Make to connect CRM (Customer Relationship Management), email, and spreadsheets.
- Tools: HubSpot AI, Apollo AI, Grammarly, Typefully, Clay, Zapier/Make, Looker Studio.

## 4. Common Mistakes & Fixes

- Mistake: Over-automation. Fix: Keep personalized touches and final reviews.
- Mistake: Poor data sync. Fix: Ensure CRMs and tools share clean fields and IDs.
- Mistake: Off-brand tone. Fix: Create a style guide and examples in your prompt library.

## 5. Hands-on Exercises

- Guided: Build one automation: incoming lead → enrich → route → notify → create task.
- Guided: Create 5 email/post templates and test with a control group.
- Challenge: Automate call summarization and track accuracy and time saved across 10 calls.
